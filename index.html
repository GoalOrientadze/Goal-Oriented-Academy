About Us

At Goal-Oriented Academy, we primarily offer courses in two directions:

  • Major: Programming
  • Minor: Management + Marketing + Development of Contemporary Skills
  • Graphic Design and 3D Modeling

Both courses encompass a thorough understanding of all relevant technologies for the 2030s. We begin with learning Python and prepare students for future tech interviews.

Our educational approach is built on the "4C" principle:

  1. Connect: After introducing the topic and problem, students must pose specific questions and answer them with their knowledge.
  2. Construct: Each task includes construction or assembly activities to conduct experiments collaboratively.
  3. Contemplate: Students reflect on what they have learned and share insights with each other.
  4. Continue: Every task and assignment concludes with a new set of challenges based on acquired knowledge to keep students motivated and passionate about learning.
